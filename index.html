Hi I'm Ruka!

I'm a third year student at King's College London studying Economics and Management. Outside of my studies I enjoy being creative. Photography, painting and making cards are some of the most recent ways that I have been exploring my creativity.

Discover Le Wagon

Photography

Wherever I go, I try to take photos of the places and people around me. I love how moments and memories can be captured on camera.

Painting

From time to time I enjoy painting. I find it relaxing and I love seeing the end product come together. I gift my paintings to friends and family or use them to decorate my room.

Card Making

I like adding a personal touch to the cards that I give to people, so I make my own. It allows me to personalise them with their photos and make unique designs. I enjoy making them and they are always very well received.
